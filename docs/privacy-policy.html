AeriMed – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

AeriMed ("the App") is a private medical protocols and team coordination tool intended for use by authorized personnel only. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Patient care reports – medical information entered manually into the patient report forms (MIST, SOAP, TCCC).
  • Certifications and licenses – images or PDFs of professional credentials you upload to the Cert Vault feature.
  • Callsign and user identifier – a display name and a randomly generated device ID used to identify your device within a team session.

Information collected automatically

  • Location data – GPS coordinates are collected while the Tactical Map feature is active. Location is shared in real time with other members of your active team session. Location data is not collected when the Tactical Map is closed.
  • Microphone audio – the speech-to-text feature temporarily processes audio to transcribe dictated notes. Audio is not stored or transmitted.
  • Camera / photo library – images captured or selected for Cert Vault are stored locally on your device and, if Supabase sync is configured, uploaded to your organization's private Supabase storage bucket.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display and synchronize team positions on the Tactical Map.
  • To store and transmit patient care reports to authorized administrators within your organization.
  • To allow administrators to view team members' professional certifications for credentialing and compliance purposes.
  • To distribute medical protocols from administrators to authorized users.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third-party advertisers or data brokers.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in two locations:

  • On-device – patient reports, certifications, and app settings are stored locally using your device's secure storage.
  • Supabase – if configured by your organization's administrator, certain data (patient reports, certifications, protocol files, Tactical Map positions) is synchronized to a Supabase database instance controlled by your organization. Supabase data is hosted on servers provided by Supabase Inc. and subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

On-device data is retained until you delete it within the App or uninstall the App. Data stored in your organization's Supabase project is retained according to your organization's data management policies and can be deleted by your administrator.

5. Access Controls

The App uses an administrator credential system to restrict access to administrative functions. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their credentials.

6. Children's Privacy

AeriMed is intended for use by trained medical and emergency response professionals. It is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information held about you. To exercise these rights, contact your organization's administrator or reach us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
